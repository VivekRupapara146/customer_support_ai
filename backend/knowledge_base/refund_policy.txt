Refund Policy

TechMart Electronics offers a 30-day return window on all products purchased directly from our store. To request a refund, the item must be in its original packaging and unused condition. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or our error. Premium subscription fees are refundable within 7 days of purchase if the service has not been used.

International Orders
International orders follow the same 30-day return window, but the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived defective. Customs duties and import taxes paid at delivery are non-refundable, as these are collected by the destination country, not TechMart.

Partial Refunds
If a returned item shows minor signs of use (e.g., a removed protective film) but is otherwise functional and complete, a partial refund of up to 85% of the purchase price may be issued at the discretion of the support team, rather than a full refund or outright rejection.

Gift Returns
Items received as gifts can be returned for store credit without needing the original purchaser's payment information, provided the item is within the 30-day window from the original purchase date (not the gift delivery date) and the gift receipt or order number is available.

Damaged or Defective Items on Arrival
If an item arrives visibly damaged or is defective out of the box, report it within 48 hours of delivery for an expedited replacement or full refund, including original shipping costs. Items reported after 48 days but within the standard 30-day window are handled under the standard defect process, which may take longer to resolve.
