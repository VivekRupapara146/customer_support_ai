Complaint Escalation Process

TechMart Electronics takes all customer complaints seriously. If you are not satisfied with a product or service, your complaint will first be reviewed by our support team within 24 hours. If unresolved, complaints are escalated to a senior support specialist within 3 business days. For complaints involving billing disputes over $100 or repeated unresolved issues, customers may request escalation directly to a account manager. All complaints are logged and tracked with a reference number provided to the customer at first contact.

Escalation Tiers
Tier 1 (initial support) handles most requests and can approve refunds, replacements, and standard troubleshooting. Tier 2 (senior specialist) handles disputes, policy exceptions, and complaints unresolved after 3 business days at Tier 1. Tier 3 (account manager) is reserved for high-value disputes, repeated complaints from the same account, or cases involving potential legal or regulatory concern.

What to Include When Filing a Complaint
Complaints are resolved faster when they include the order number, a description of what went wrong, and what resolution is being requested (refund, replacement, credit, etc.). Complaints filed without an order number may take longer to resolve while the team locates the relevant account and order history.

Response Time Expectations
Standard complaints receive an initial response within 24 hours and a resolution or clear next-step update within 3 business days. Complaints escalated to Tier 3 may take up to 10 business days for full resolution due to the additional review required, though the customer receives status updates every 3 business days during this period.

Unsatisfactory Resolution
If a customer remains unsatisfied after a Tier 3 review, TechMart offers a final internal review by a different account manager not previously involved in the case. This is the final internal escalation step; further disputes would need to go through the customer's applicable consumer protection channels, which TechMart's support team can provide information about upon request.
