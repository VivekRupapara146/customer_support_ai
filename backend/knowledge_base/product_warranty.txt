Product Warranty and Specifications

All TechMart Electronics products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty plans of up to 3 years are available for purchase at checkout. Warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs. Product specification sheets, including compatibility information, are available on each product's page. If a product is discontinued, compatible replacement parts remain available for 2 years after the discontinuation date.

Filing a Warranty Claim
To file a warranty claim, provide the product's serial number and original proof of purchase. Claims are typically reviewed within 3 business days. Approved claims result in either a repair, a replacement unit, or store credit at TechMart's discretion, depending on part availability. Refurbished replacement units may be issued for products older than 6 months if a new unit is unavailable; refurbished replacements carry the remainder of the original warranty term, not a new full term.

Extended Warranty Coverage Details
The extended warranty (1-3 additional years) covers the same defects as the standard warranty and additionally includes one accidental damage claim per year (e.g., a single drop or liquid spill), subject to a service fee equal to 15% of the product's original price. Extended warranty must be purchased within 30 days of the original product purchase; it cannot be added later.

International Warranty Coverage
Standard warranty coverage is honored internationally through TechMart's authorized service partners, though repair turnaround times may be longer outside the country of purchase. Extended warranty plans purchased in one country may not transfer if the product is later used in another country — check with support before relying on this coverage while traveling long-term.

Compatibility and Accessories
Official TechMart accessories are tested for compatibility with the specific product generation they're listed for; using accessories across generations (e.g., an older charger with a newer device) is not guaranteed to work and is not covered under warranty if it causes damage.
