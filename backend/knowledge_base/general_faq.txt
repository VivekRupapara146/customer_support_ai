General FAQ — Store Hours, Shipping, and Account Basics

Store Hours and Contact
TechMart Electronics customer support is available Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 8 PM, and Saturday through Sunday, 10 AM to 6 PM, in your local time zone. Live chat support is available during all listed hours. Email support is monitored continuously and typically responded to within 24 hours.

Shipping Timelines
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental region. Expedited shipping (1-2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. International shipping timelines vary by destination and are shown at checkout before purchase is confirmed. Orders placed before 2 PM local time on a business day typically ship the same day; orders placed after 2 PM or on a weekend ship the next business day.

Order Tracking
A tracking number is emailed automatically once an order ships. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to become active in the carrier's system after the email is sent. If a tracking number shows no movement for more than 5 business days, contact support with your order number.

Creating and Managing Your Account
To create an account, use the "Sign Up" option with a valid email address; no purchase is required to register. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page to receive a reset email. Account email addresses can be changed from the account settings page after verifying your current password. Deleting an account is permanent and removes order history; contact support if you need order records preserved before deletion.

Loyalty Program
TechMart Rewards members earn points on every purchase, redeemable for discounts on future orders. Points do not expire as long as the account remains active (at least one purchase or login within 12 months). Reward points are not refundable for cash and are forfeited if an order is fully refunded.
